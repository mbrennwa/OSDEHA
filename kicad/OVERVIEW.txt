OSDEHA PCBs / boards

A complete set of OSDEHA boards consists of the following:

2 x amplifier boards (including DHT filament CCS)
1 x power supply board (HV supply, raw DHT filament supply, heater supply, HV power-on delay)
1 x volume control board (different versions for different pots: Alps RK27, Khozmo, etc.)

In addition:
1 x mains transformer for raw DHT filament supply, heaters supply, and HV power-on delay with mains switch relay for HV transformer
1 x mains transformer for HV supply
